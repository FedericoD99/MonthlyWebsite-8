Game Instructions

Welcome to Marvel Battle Arena! Select your heroes and battle enemies or another player in turn-based combat.

Controls:
  • Attack Button: Deal damage to a random enemy.
  • Special Button: Use a hero's unique special ability (e.g., stun, heal, multi-target).
  • Special Info Button: View a character's special ability details.
Game Modes:
  • Final Boss Mode: Defeat three waves of enemies, culminating in a battle against Thanos (single or multiplayer).
  • Infinite Mode: Survive endless waves of enemies with increasing difficulty (single or multiplayer).
  • Multiplayer Mode: Compete in a best-of-three match against another player's heroes (multiplayer only).
Powerups:

After each wave (except in Multiplayer Mode), choose one of three powerups to boost your heroes.

  • Health Boost: Restores 20 health to all heroes (max 100).
  • Attack Boost: Increases attack by 5 for all heroes.
  • Shield: Grants a one-turn shield to all heroes, blocking one attack.
Objective:

Reduce all enemy health to zero to win a wave. In multiplayer mode, win three waves to claim victory. In cooperative modes, work together to defeat enemies!
