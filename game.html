    Game Instructions

    Assemble your heroes in Marvel Cosmic Arena and face epic battles!

    Controls:
    • Attack Button: Deal damage to a random enemy.
    • Special Button: Use powerful abilities (mana/cooldowns apply).
    • Info Button: View ability details.
    • Keyboard: 'A' for attack, 'S' for special.
    Game Modes:
    • Final Boss Mode: Defeat Thanos in three waves.
    • Infinite Mode: Survive endless waves.
    • Multiplayer Mode: Best-of-three match.
    Powerups:

    Choose powerups after waves to boost heroes.

    Scoring:

    Earn points for defeating enemies, using specials, and clearing waves.
